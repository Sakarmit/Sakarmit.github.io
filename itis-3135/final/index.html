Welcome! To Game Developers @ UNC Charlotte

We are a club based around the understanding and creation of games in many formats. We host or participate in at least two game jams a semester, with the rest of the meetings spent on understanding and applying game development concepts.


The Four Jams

  • Kick off Game Jam (Month Long)
  • Fall Game Jam (49 hours)
  • Global Game Jam (49 hours)
  • Spring Game Jam (49 hours)

This club welcomes everyone, not just programmers but artists, writers, managers, etc.


Come meet people with a variety of interests but the same love for games.
