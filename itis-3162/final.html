Classes Of UNC Charlotte


Introduction

Course Registration the period of stress and confusion every college student faces each semester. Will they get the classes they desire or most they settle for a bad class or non at all. During this project we will look at which factors impact the fill ratio of a class the most? Can we find correlation between element which can tell us which classes students should likely have backups because of them being likely to fill up the most.


About the Data

The dataset we will use for this project was created by me. I used a open api endpoint on UNC Charlotte banner self service to collect the data using javascript and storing in csv format. The is data is of all classes offered at UNCC from the Spring 2024 semester as of 12/24/2023. There are 52 main columns of data with total of 5712 rows each being a unique section of classes. Of the columns ones that may be of intrest for this project are:

  • partOfTerm: Full, First Half or Second Half
  • scheduleTypeDescription: Lecture, Internship Independent Study, Lab etc.
  • subjectCourse: acronym representing the subject of the course (ie. ACCT for accounting) and course number within the respective subject
  • maximumEnrollment & enrollment: max students that can register and how many are registered
  • creditHourSession: credits you can earn from the course
  • sectionAttributes: any special attributes a class may have (ie. Gen ed requirement)
  • instructionalMethod: how the class is held face-to-face, online async, online specific time etc
  • instructor: who is teaching the course
  • meetingBuilding: where the class takes place
  • meetingBeginTime & endTime: period the class takes during
  • {days of the week}: each representing whether that class is held that day of the week
